Privacy Policy


Information we collect about you

You only provide us your public Twitter profile via the "Log In With Twitter" functionnality, this is the only information we store about you.

We do not access nor read your Tweets, Lists, Subscribers list. We might at some point add a option to easily post a link to our website to your Twitter feed.

When subscribing to a list, you are redirected to our payment processor Stripe which will ask you for your name, address and credit card information. We do not retrieve nor store these information, but we can still access them on Stripe tools in order to handle refunds or disputes. We do not and will not transfer them to other third-party, but we advice you to read Stripe's global privacy policy and the specific privacy policy of your country of residence if you want to know more about what Stripe does with the information provided.

We do not store any information attached to your device, location, IP address, nor share them with any third party.

We do not use any amalytic tools for now, but we might use one in the future, but no private information will be shared with this tools, which will only anonymously track our product usage.
